Turn on from the plug socket on the wall.

Turn on Sky box.

Sky box takes at least 2 minutes to start, so please be patient.

Press TV on the remote and press the channel up to turn it on. However ensure channel EXT1 is selected.

Press the SKY button on the remote to switch control to the Sky box. Use tvguide on the remote for an overview.

To save power we recommend turning both TV+Sky off at the wall when not in use.

Careful moving the TV since the SCART connector is very tight and if loose the picture will degrade.
