Location

The cottage is situated in the center of Cornwall in the South West of England, UK.

Conveniently positioned for you to explore North, South, East and West of Cornwall.

  • Google map of Praze Farm

Getting here:

By plane:

Closest commercial Airport is Newquay NQY

If you have a small plane, you could land it at Bodmin airport and easily walk to Praze Farm.

By car (recommended):

Take the A30 from Exeter to Cornwall. Travel past Launceston. After passing Jamaica Inn, lookout for the sign indicating Millpool/Cardinham to the left. Take that turning down a narrow country road, you will find Praze Farm as the property to your right. Enter with the second/bottom entrance where the sign is. If you pass Bodmin you have gone too far.

You can look up maps and detailed driving instructions with our postcode PL30 4HZ. Please give us a call before you arrive if possible.

By train:

The closest station about 15 minutes away by car is Bodmin Parkway station (BOD). Roughly 4 hours away from London Paddington station (PAD).
